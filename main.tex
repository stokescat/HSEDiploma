	\begin{abstractpagerus}
		Данная работа посвящена анализу роли выпускных квалификационных работ в системе высшего образования XXI века. 	\end{abstractpagerus}
	
	\begin{abstractpageeng}
		This work is devoted to the analysis of the role of final qualification works in the higher education system of the 21st century. Historical prerequisites, modern requirements, and practical aspects of diploma projects in the field of software engineering are considered. Special attention is paid to the integration of scientific research and real industrial tasks.
	\end{abstractpageeng}
